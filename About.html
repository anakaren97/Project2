Our Story

We start our journey in 2003, Vancouver. The name our massage "AC" is from the two founders' capital letters of their first name: Ana and Chang. Ana and Chang frustrated with the limits that the standard approach to traditional massage imposed. They wanted to be able to offer new forms of massage therapies that would provide more well-rounded care for their clients.
