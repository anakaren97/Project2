Description

Swedish massage is effective for reducing symptoms of stress, anxiety, fatigue, muscular tension, mental stress and can improve the body's circulatory system. The techniques involve long strokes, tapping, shaking motions, friction tapping and kneading where the pressure is applied between the bones and muscles.
